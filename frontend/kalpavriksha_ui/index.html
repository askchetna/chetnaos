About This Suite

Kalpavriksha ("wish-fulfilling tree" in Sanskrit) is ChetnaOS's agricultural intelligence module. It applies heuristic models trained on Indian soil science, plantation economics, and climate data to help farmers and agribusiness professionals make smarter land-use decisions — entirely offline and without an internet connection.
